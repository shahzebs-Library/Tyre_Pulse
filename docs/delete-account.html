Account & Data Deletion

TyrePulse Inspector · Effective 13 July 2026

TyrePulse Inspector accounts are created and managed by each organization's administrator. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data as described below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email your-support-email@example.com from the address associated with your account, or include your username / employee ID and organization name.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my account".
  3. We verify the request with your organization's administrator and action it within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and login credentials.
  • Personal profile data: name, email, employee ID, phone, and push-notification token.

What may be retained

Operational records you created (e.g. tyre inspections, accident reports, meter and stock entries) may be retained by your organization for legitimate fleet-management, safety, and legal-compliance purposes, where permitted by law. Where retained, such records are dissociated from your personal contact details where feasible.
