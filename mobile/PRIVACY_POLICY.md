# Privacy Policy — TyrePulse Inspector

**Last updated: 13 July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the **independent developer of TyrePulse
Inspector** ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information in the
**TyrePulse Inspector** mobile application ("the App"). TyrePulse Inspector is an internal workforce tool
used by fleet operators to record tyre inspections, meter readings, stock counts,
and accident reports. Accounts are provisioned by your organization's
administrator; the App is not intended for the general public or for children.

If you have questions, contact us at **info@tyrepulse.app**.

---

## 1. Information we collect

We collect only what the App needs to do its job:

**a) Account & identity information**
Name, email address, username, employee ID, assigned role, site, and country —
used to authenticate you and attribute the records you create. Accounts are
created by your administrator; the App has no public sign-up.

**b) Location information (precise)**
When you perform a tyre inspection, the App may capture your device's GPS
coordinates to geo-tag where the inspection was carried out. Location is used
only at the moment of an inspection and only if you grant the permission; if it
is unavailable or denied, the inspection still saves without coordinates.

**c) Photos and signatures**
Photos you capture (tyres, accident scenes, odometer/hour-meter gauges) and
signatures you draw are stored as part of the related record.

**d) Operational records you create**
Inspections, checklists, accident reports, meter and stock entries, work notes,
and similar content you enter in the App.

**e) Device and diagnostic information**
A push-notification token (to deliver alerts to your device) and crash/error and
performance diagnostics (via Sentry), which may include device model, operating
system version, and a technical device/install identifier. This is used to keep
the App reliable.

We do **not** collect financial information, health data, contacts, messages,
browsing history, or advertising identifiers, and the App contains **no ads**.

---

## 2. How we use information
- To authenticate you and secure access to your organization's data.
- To record, display, and analyze fleet, tyre, inspection, accident, meter, and
  stock information.
- To geo-tag inspections (when permitted).
- To send operational notifications relevant to your role.
- To diagnose crashes and improve reliability and performance.

We do **not** sell your personal information and do **not** use it for
advertising or cross-app tracking.

---

## 3. How information is shared
We share information only with service providers ("processors") that operate the
App on our behalf, under contract and only as needed:
- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and file storage (backend).
- **Sentry** — crash and performance diagnostics.
- **Expo / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** — delivery of push notifications.

We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect legal rights.
We do not otherwise share your data with third parties for their own purposes.

---

## 4. Data security
Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS) and stored in access-
controlled systems with organization-level isolation and role-based access
controls. Locally, credentials and offline queue data are held in the device's
secure storage. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take
reasonable measures to protect your information.

---

## 5. Data retention
We retain records for as long as your organization requires them for fleet
operations, compliance, and historical analysis, and as required by applicable
law. Diagnostic data is retained for a limited period per our provider's defaults.

---

## 6. Your rights & data deletion
Because accounts are managed by your organization:
- To **access or correct** your information, contact your fleet administrator or
  us at **info@tyrepulse.app**.
- To **request deletion** of your account and associated personal data, email
  **info@tyrepulse.app** with your name and organization. We will action
  the request subject to legal and legitimate business retention requirements.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g. under GDPR or
local law) to object to or restrict processing or to data portability.

---

## 7. Children's privacy
The App is a workforce tool intended for users **18 and older** and is not
directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by
