Privacy Policy — TyrePulse Inspector

Effective 13 July 2026

TyrePulse Inspector ("the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer ("we", "us"). The App is an internal workforce tool used by fleet operators to record tyre inspections, meter readings, stock counts, and accident reports. Accounts are provisioned by each organization's administrator; the App is not intended for the general public or for children. Contact: info@tyrepulse.app.

1. Information we collect

  • Account & identity — name, email, username, employee ID, role, site, country; used to authenticate you and attribute the records you create.
  • Precise location — GPS coordinates captured to geo-tag where a tyre inspection was performed, only when you grant permission. If denied or unavailable, the inspection still saves without coordinates.
  • Photos & signatures — images you capture (tyres, accident scenes, odometer/hour-meter gauges) and signatures you draw, stored with the related record.
  • Operational records — inspections, checklists, accident reports, meter and stock entries, and notes you enter.
  • Device & diagnostics — a push-notification token (to deliver alerts) and crash/performance diagnostics (via Sentry), which may include device model, OS version, and a technical device identifier.

We do not collect financial data, health data, contacts, messages, or browsing history, and the App contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or ad tracking.

2. How we use information

  • Authenticate you and secure access to your organization's data.
  • Record, display, and analyze fleet, tyre, inspection, accident, meter, and stock information.
  • Geo-tag inspections (when permitted) and send operational notifications.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve reliability. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. How information is shared

We share data only with service providers that operate the App on our behalf, under contract and only as needed:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage.
  • Sentry — crash and performance diagnostics.
  • Expo / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — push-notification delivery.

We may disclose information where required by law. We do not otherwise share your data with third parties for their own purposes.

4. Data security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS) and stored in access-controlled systems with organization-level isolation and role-based access. Credentials and offline data are held in the device's secure storage. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Data retention

We retain records for as long as your organization requires them for fleet operations, compliance, and historical analysis, and as required by law. Diagnostic data is retained for a limited period per our provider's defaults.

6. Your rights & data deletion

Because accounts are managed by your organization, to access or correct your information, contact your fleet administrator or us at info@tyrepulse.app. To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, see our Account & Data Deletion page. Depending on your location you may have additional rights (e.g. under GDPR) to object to or restrict processing, or to data portability.

7. Children's privacy

The App is a workforce tool for users 18 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the effective date above.
